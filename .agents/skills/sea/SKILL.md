# SEA (Self-Evolving AI) Superpower Plugin

Self-evolving adaptive system for continuous learning and improvement of AI capabilities and behavior.

- Adaptive learning algorithms and weight adjustment
- Evidence-based self-improvement
- Behavioral constitution auditing and enforcement
- Feedback collection and analysis
- Continuous model optimization

## Integration

Connects with ABI's AI constitution, SEA loops, and learning systems to provide autonomous evolution and improvement capabilities.
