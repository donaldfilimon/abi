# Code Of Conduct

Contributors are expected to communicate clearly, keep reviews factual, and prioritize a safe and respectful project environment.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct without being abusive.
- Focus on technical substance.
- Assume good faith until evidence shows otherwise.
- Respect maintainer decisions on scope, timing, and release risk.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or threats
- Doxxing or publication of private information
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of issues, reviews, or discussions

Project maintainers may moderate or remove content that violates these expectations.
